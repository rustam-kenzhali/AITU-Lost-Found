How to stop losing stuff?

Fighting pathological absent-mindedness!

There are people who remain focused under any circumstances, and some people literally suffer from their inattention. High busyness, multitasking, stress and fatigue make us more forgetful.

  • Put everything in its place. It's very wise to put everything in its place.
  • Block all attempts of the brain to switch to something else and try to hover in the clouds.
  • Don't hoard things, putting them off for later. When a task comes up, do it.
  • Do not put anything in your pockets - they will not replace a bag, but the phone can easily fall out of the pocket.
  • Be sure to put everything in a bag or bag as soon as you stop using it.
