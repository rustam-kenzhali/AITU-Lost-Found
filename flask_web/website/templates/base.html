About

Students are often in a hurry, especially in the run-up to midterms, endterms, or final exams. In the daily hustle and bustle, it's easy to forget your belongings in the classroom, in the openspace, or in the library. To make it easier to find a lost item, a website has been created to post information about lost and found items.
